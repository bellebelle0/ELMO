Characters

Elmo

Elmo is the main character for the segment "Elmo's World" in Sesame Street. He introduces the subject at the beginning of each episode and hosts the entire segment.

Dorothy

Dorothy is Elmo's pet goldfish. In Dorothy's fish bowl, there is a prop related tot he given topic of the episode. Dorothy will provide a fact and question about the topic at hand in the form of thoughts, leading to a segment with one of the Noodles.

Mr. Noodle

Mr. Noodle is a recurring character in Elmo's World. In the portion of Elmo's World where Mr. Noodle appears, he makes an attempt to answer Dorothy's question. However, he isn't the brightest person, and often uses comical ways to try and answer the question.

Mr. Noodle's Brother, Mr. Noodle

Mr. Noodle is Mr. Noodle's brother. Occasionally, he will appear in place of his brother. Other times, he will appear alongside his brother. He will also make attempts to solve Dorothy's question, whether it be by himself or with his other family members. Just like his brother, he is shown to be a very silly character who adds comedy to the show.

Mr. Noodle's Sister, Ms. Noodle

Ms. Noodle is the sister to Mr. Noodle and Mr. Noodle's Brother, Mr. Noodle. She is a lot like her two brothers and is also very comical. She appears in the show with her brothers or by herself, making funny attempts to answer Elmo's questions.
